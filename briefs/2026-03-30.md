## Gameplan

**Environment:** Defensive rotation. Money moving out of growth (Tech, Discretionary) into safety (Energy, Utilities, Staples). Strong dollar adding pressure on international earnings. VIX at 30+ signals elevated uncertainty.

**If in cash:** Stay patient. 15/24 global indexes bearish — odds favor waiting. Watch for MAs to flatten and begin rising as the earliest sign of a turn.

**If in positions:** Relative strength is in defensive sectors (Energy, Utilities, Staples). Risk is highest in growth sectors (Tech, Discretionary, Financials).

**What would change this:**
- S&P 500 / Nasdaq reclaiming the 50-day MA — first sign of stabilization
- Major US indexes shifting from DOWNTREND to MIXED — bleeding slowing
- VIX dropping below its 50-day MA — fear subsiding

**Bottom line:** Broadly weak, globally. Energy is the standout exception. Caution over aggression until regime checks start flipping.
